FYWA

F*ck You On About?

A chaotic speaker-vs-guessers party game.

How to Play

  1. Join a room with a nickname and room code.
  2. One player becomes the speaker each round.
  3. The speaker sees the secret topic and 7 allowed letters.
  4. Guessers see the letters but not the topic.
  5. Speaker describes the topic in Discord or voice chat.
  6. Guessers buzz in when they know the answer.
  7. Wrong answer = frozen out for the round.
  8. Correct answer = guesser and speaker both score.
  9. If the speaker messes up, everyone else scores.
