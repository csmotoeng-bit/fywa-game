F*ck You On About?

A chaotic voice-chat party game about describing absolute nonsense with limited letters.

How to Play

  1. At the start of each turn, everyone votes for the category.
  2. One turn means every player gets one round as speaker.
  3. The speaker sees the secret topic and 7 allowed letters.
  4. Letters can be used in any order.
  5. Guessers see the allowed letters too, but not the topic.
  6. Buzz in when you know the answer.
  7. The round timer pauses while someone is guessing.
  8. Wrong answer means you are frozen until the letters reroll.
  9. Each speaker gets a panic button to change their prompt once per game.
